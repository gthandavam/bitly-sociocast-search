What it's about

Bit.ly takes in phrases and gives back URLs. Sociocast takes in URLs and gives back categories.

To a user, phrases and categories are the same: they are all topics. Our system will take in a topic from the user, and will give them back what that topic means in terms of other topics, as well as further reading.
